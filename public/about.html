Frequently asked questions

What is MedWay and how does it work?
MedWay is a medical AI tool that analyzes symptoms and health documents to generate easy-to-understand reports. You simply upload your files or input symptoms, and our AI processes the information to give you smart insights—fast.
Is my personal health data safe with MedWay?
Yes. Privacy is a top priority. All your medical information is encrypted, securely stored, and never shared without your consent. You’re always in full control of your data.
Do I need a medical background to use MedWay?
Not at all. MedWay is designed for everyone. The interface is simple, and the reports are written in plain language—no medical jargon, just clear insights.
Can I use MedWay for someone else, like a family member?
Yes. You can upload records or input symptoms on behalf of a family member or loved one. Just make sure you have their permission to handle their data responsibly.
What kinds of documents can I upload?
You can upload PDFs, scanned medical reports, lab results, prescriptions, or discharge summaries. The AI is trained to recognize and summarize various healthcare formats.
